Dammam is the capital city of the eastern province of Saudi Arabia. Dammam is located on the Arabian Gulf and has a population of 1.5 million people. However, Dammam is located in the Greater Dammam area which has the three cities of Dammam, Khobar, and Dhahran. The Greater Dammam Area has a population of 2.2 million and is the center of the Saudi oil industry. Dammam is the fastest growing city in Saudi Arabia!

Jeddah is port city located on the red sea on the west side of Saudi Arabia. Jeddah has the biggest port in Saudi Arabia and is the commerical center of the county. It has a population of 3.7 million people and is the second largest city in the country. Jeddah is known for it seaside resorts and booming entertainment industry. Currently the Formula 1 Saudi Grand prix is located in Jeddah!
